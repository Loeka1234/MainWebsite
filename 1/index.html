Welcome to Xiler

Xiler is a network of servers and bots, these are all connected! We offer loads of custom features and want to offer the best quality services in the market, as we believe that quality over quantity is the way to go! To provide as best of an experience to you we offer really great support at our discord (dc.xiler.net).

What do we offer?

We offer a variety of servers which will only expand as time goes on. You'll definitely have a great experience here, and if you don't contact our network managers/owners so we can try to make your experience as best as possible as long as your request is reasonable! Our bots are using a function which no bot currently offers on the market, that function is using extensions. These extensions can execute a specific part very well. If you for example want music in your discord server you don't have to invite all Xiler bots, just the music bot. Adding the main bot is heavily recommended as all bots communicate with each other through our API, this way if you set the prefix eg to - then the other (Xiler) bots that are in the server will also change their prefix.
